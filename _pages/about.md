Welcome
======
Hello!  I am an Assistant Professor of Legal Studies in the Department of Management at the Spears School of Business at Oklahoma State University.  My research focuses on using quantitative methods to conduct empirical studies in the areas of law and ethics, employment law, and intellectual property law.  My scholarly work has appeared or is forthcoming in the _American Business Law Journal_, _Cornell Law Review_, _DePaul Law Review_, _West Virginia Law Review_, _Pace Law Review_, _Georgetown Journal of Legal Ethics_, _International Studies Quarterly_, and _International Affairs_.

I earned my Ph.D. in political science from Northwestern University and my J.D., _cum laude_, from Cornell Law School, where I served as an Articles Editor on the _Cornell Law Review_.  I am a past recipient of the university-wide Merrick Foundation Teaching Award at Oklahoma State University.  I also previously received the Holmes-Cardozo Award for the best submitted conference paper at the Academy of Legal Studies in Business Annual Conference.  I currently serve as a Staff Editor at the _American Business Law Journal_ and as the Vice President of the Employment Law Section of the Academy of Legal Studies in Business.
